Amandeep did her “Bachelors in Science” and “Masters in Microbiology” from India after which she worked for 2 years as a Research Assistant at Institute of Microbial technology (CSIR-IMTECH, India) and gained experience in morpho-molecular identification and phylogeny of filamentous fungi. She moved to the University of South Wales (USW) in 2010 to pursue doctoral studies on Microbial Fuel Cells (MFCs) based sensor under EPSRC funded SUPERGEN Biofuel cell consortium Project.
